Cannoli filling

Cannoli with the ricotta filling can be rich and deliciously sweetened, and those little flecks of mini dark chocolate chips swirled throughout just amp up that already immensely satisfying flavor.

These delectable Italian delicacies are truly worth learning to make!

You will need some cannoli for this recipe, you can buy or do your own.

Ingredients:

  • Cannolis pre-made.
  • Whole milk ricotta.
  • Powdered Sugar at taste.
  • Chocolate chips at taste.
  • Pinch of cinnamon.

Steps:

  1. Strain the ricotta.
  2. Add ricotta, sugar, chocolate chips and cinnamon to a mixing bowl.
  3. Stir mixture to blend.
  4. Put it inside your pre-made Cannolis.
