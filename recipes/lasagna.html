Lasagna

Lasagna is both a type of pasta made up of wide strips, as well as a dish, sometimes called oven-baked lasagna.

Generally, the mass has these ribbons placed in layers, and interspersed with stuffing and sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 500g lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 boxes of sour cream
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 500g of ham
  • 500g of mozzarella
  • salt to taste
  • 2 glasses of milk (400mL)
  • 1 grated onion
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 box of tomato sauce
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 packet of grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the manufacturer's directions.
  2. Pour it in a refractory with ice water so it doesn't stick and set it aside.
  3. Meanwhile, sauté the garlic, onion, ground beef, tomato sauce, cook for 3 minutes and set aside.
  4. For the white sauce, melt the margarine, add the 3 tablespoons of wheat flour and stir.
  5. Slowly pour in the milk and continue stirring.
  6. Finally, add the cream, stir for 1 minute and turn off the heat.
  7. When assembling, pour a part of the bolognese sauce in a refractory, half of the pasta, half of the ham, half of the mozzarella, all the white sauce and the rest of the pasta.
  8. the layers to the edge of the container.
  9. with the grated cheese and bake in a high oven (220° C), preheated, for about 20 minutes.
